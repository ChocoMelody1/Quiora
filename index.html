Quiora

Native to the land of Sildrea, Quiora have adapted to the many environments Sildrea has to offer. No one has been able to say where the Quiora originate from, but many believe the Astral Sea is the best candidate.

Quiora are sometimes confused with elves, specifically Drow. In reality, Quiora are a completely different race, although interracial mingling between the two is becoming much more common.

Quiora are known for their levitational ability and their relationship to the night, moon and stars. Quiora are a peaceful, political race, and are accepting of other religions, which has caused Sildrea to have a wide range of religions.

Quiora are characterised by floppy ears, pale eyes, and tall statures. Their skin is shades of purple, and hair ranges from white to pale blonde.

Quiora Attributes.

Creature type-

You are a humanoid. you are also considered a Celestial for any prerequisites or effects that requires you to be a celestial.

Age-

Quiorians live for a long time, longer than most races. You reach adulthood at 50 years old, and live to 500.

Size-

Most Quiorians range from 5ft to 7ft. Your size is medium.

Speed-

Your walking speed is 30ft

Ability score increases-

You gain a +2 to your Intelligence score and a +1 to your Charisma score

Alignment-

Most Quiorians lean toward good and neutrality, as well as the lawful side of the alignment spectrum. Chaotic and evil alignments are more common amongst Quiorians that grow up outside Quiorian circles.

Levitation-

Quiora have the ability to levitate. You can walk if you choose to. You must be up to three feet off stable, solid ground; conscious and upright to use this ability.you use your walking speed when you use this ability. Difficult terrain does affect you.

Eyes of the moon-

You have darkvision out to 120 ft, seeing dim light as bright light, and darkness as dim light. You see this world in shades of bluish-purple.

Light of the night-

You learn the dancing lights cantrip. At third level, you learn colour spray and at fifth level you learn moonbeam. You can cast these spells without using a spell slot once per long rest. You choose whether you use intelligence or wisdom as your spellcasting ability.

Languages-

You can speak Common, Quiorian, and one other language of your choice.
